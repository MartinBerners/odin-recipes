Taco made on minced meat

Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Taco seasoning
  • Salsa sauce
  • Tortilla
  • lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Peppers
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Heat up a large frying pan and brown the minced meat with the taco seasoning.
  2. Wash the vegetables and chop them int small pieces.
  3. Heat up the tortilla in microwave.
  4. Put the chopped vegetables and the cooked meat in the tortilla and eat.
